AI Investigation : Introduction to Copilots, Microsoft Copilot and Copilot Studio

Created by Diego Andres Bolanos Osejo (Unlicensed), last modified on Jun 03, 2024

What are Copilots

Copilots are generative AI assistants integrated into applications, often as chat interfaces. They provide contextualized support for everyday tasks in those applications.

Levels of Copilot adoption

You can generally categorize industry and personal Copilot adoption into three buckets: off-the-shelf use, extending Microsoft Copilot, and custom development.

  • You can use off-the-shelf copilots, like Microsoft Copilot for Microsoft 365, to empower users and increase their productivity.

  • You can extend Microsoft Copilot to support custom business processes or tasks, using your data to control how Copilot responds to user prompts in your organization.

  • You can build custom copilots to integrate generative AI into business apps or create unique customer experiences.

Microsoft Copilot is integrated into various Microsoft applications and user experiences.

Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft Copilot integrates AI capabilities into various Microsoft applications to enhance productivity, safeguard businesses, and extend AI functionalities. Key uses include:

  • Web Browsing: Integrated with Microsoft Edge and Bing for content creation and research.

  • Productivity Apps: Embedded in Microsoft 365 (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook) to assist with document generation, presentations, and email management.

  • Business Processes: Integrated into Dynamics 365 for customer service, sales, and supply chain optimization.

  • Data Analytics: Supports data analysis in Microsoft Fabric and Power BI.

  • IT and Security: Aids in managing IT infrastructure and security via Azure and Microsoft Copilot for Security.

  • Software Development: GitHub Copilot helps developers with code generation and documentation.

Copilot Studio

Copilot Studio is designed to work well for low-code development scenarios in which technically proficient business users or developers can create conversational AI experiences. The resulting copilot is a fully managed SaaS (software as a service) solution hosted in your Microsoft 365 environment and delivered through chat channels like Microsoft Teams. With Copilot Studio, the infrastructure considerations and model deployment details are taken care of for you, making it easy to focus on creating an effective solution.

Microsoft Copilot Studio Capabilities

  1. Generate Conversations: Enhance AI's ability to answer questions using organizational websites and internal knowledge.

  1. Turn Conversation into Action: Use Power Platform connectors, flows, custom topics, and AI prompts for action design.

  1. Bring Systems to Life: Use prebuilt data connectors across Microsoft and non-Microsoft sources.

  1. Bring Your Own Azure Models: Integrate and train Azure services and language models.

  1. Control Responses for Specific Topics: Design predictable workflows for sensitive or regulated scenarios.

  1. Handle Complex Queries: Utilize conversational design tools for intricate conversations.

  1. Offer Personalized Interaction: Provide dynamic, real-time responses and notifications.

  1. Build the Way You Want: Create copilots using natural language, graphical interface, or code view.

  1. Expand Reach: Distribute copilots across multiple channels.

  1. Extend Line-of-Business Apps: Enhance business applications with modern conversational experiences.

  1. Use with Low-Code Apps: Connect with low-code apps, workflows, and analytics.

  1. Escalate Conversations: Continue dialogues with human agents through a customer engagement platform.

Data sources

External resources

  • AI general knowledge

  • Bing Web Search (doesn't require external configuration)

  • Bing Custom Search (requires external configuration)

Internal resources

  • SharePoint (only specific file formats are supported)

  • OneDrive

  • Documents uploaded to Dataverse

  • Custom data (internal or external): supply your source, such as a Power Automate Flow or from Skill.

Customize Copilot for Microsoft 365 Capabilities

  • Create Copilot GPTs and Plugins: Design extensions with prompts to access business data.

  • Handle Mission-Critical Scenarios: Tailor conversations for compliance and regulatory needs.

  • Boost Productivity: Automate workflows for tasks like expense submissions and employee onboarding.

  • Utilize Data: Build plugins using over 1,200 connectors, including Salesforce and ServiceNow.

Use One Connect Platform Capabilities

  • Centralized Management: Policies, access controls, and environment management.

  • Comprehensive Analytics: Built-in analytics for optimization.

  • Flexible SaaS: Managed infrastructure for peak performance.

  • Responsible AI: Governance and data control standards.

Azure AI Studio

Azure AI Studio is a PasS (platform as a service) development portal for professional software developers that gives you full control over the language model you want to use, including the capability to fine-tune the model with your own data. You can define prompt flows that orchestrate conversation flow and integrate your own data augmentation and prompt engineering logic. You can deploy the resulting copilot service in the cloud and consume it from custom-developed apps and services.

Overview

Azure AI Studio simplifies generative AI development by offering a model catalog for browsing AI models and API services, tools for crafting AI solutions, responsible AI technologies, and scalable AI deployment options.

Benefits

  • Improve Customer Experiences: Personalize content with natural language interfaces.

  • Reduce Organizational Risk: Use advanced analytics to mitigate risks.

  • Enhance Work Quality: Improve operational efficiencies and collaboration.

  • Boost Productivity: Optimize processes and automate tasks.

Capabilities

  • Build Custom Copilots: Create AI assistants for specific needs.

  • Enterprise Chat: Develop chatbots with custom data responses.

  • Multimodal Models: Combine text, images, video, and audio data.

  • Speech Analysis: Transform voice data into actionable insights.
