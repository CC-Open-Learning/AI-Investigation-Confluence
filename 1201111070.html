To create “context” for the LLM; we have to save those data into a Knowledge Base. The following are the steps to do it:

  • Website’s links [to WebBased Loader]

  • Chunks [ to RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter(700,200)]

  • Embeddings [to OllamaEmbeddings(model=”mxbai-embed-large")]

  • Vector DataBase [FAISS]

  • Retriever [named - FAQ retriever | k=5(retrieves 5 most relevant docs/chunks)]
