Created by Hamna Ashraf, last modified by Jongeon on Jan 09, 2025

Currently the transcript for the course Information Technology Operation, MGMT8680 taught by Sean Yo is taken as the course material for the chatbot.

Here is a brief explanation of what level 2 is:

This level is student-centric designed to assist learners by summarizing key course content and answering questions based on material discussed in class for a specific course.

The chatbot leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate, contextually relevant responses. It retrieves documents based on the user's query and generates answers strictly within the scope of the provided course material. This approach ensures that responses are limited to class content, avoiding irrelevant information or external knowledge not covered in the course.

Example of Level 2 questions:

  • What is change management?

  • Explain ITIL in detail.

  • Summarize Unit 2.1 Topic 1

  • What is Unit 5 about?

  • Please explain the topic titled ‘Benefits of Project Management’

Detailed explanation regarding the current approach for chatbot and other related information can be found here New Approach (CCchat)

Questions That Won’t Work:

  • Summarize Unit 9 Topic 1 - Part 1 or Part 2

This type of query will not work because the current implementation does not include checks or logic to handle further subdivisions of topics into parts (e.g., "Part 1" or "Part 2") for document retrieval.
