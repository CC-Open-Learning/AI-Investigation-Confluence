AI Investigation : Weekly Meeting Schedule

Created by Eunie, last modified on Jan 28, 2025

  1. Planning Meeting

  • When: Every Monday (10 AM)

  • Purpose: Plan tasks and set goals for the week.

  1. Review and Retrospective Meeting

  • When: Every Friday (10 AM)

  • Purpose: Review the progress of tasks, discuss completed work, and reflect on improvements.

  1. Daily Stand-up Meeting

  • When: Every day (10 AM)

  • Purpose: Share daily updates, blockers, and plans for the day.

  1. Data Acquaintance Meeting

  • When: Every Tuesday (3 PM)

  • Purpose: Discuss data-related tasks, updates, and issues.

  1. Sprint Demo Meeting

  • When: Every two weeks (Bi-weekly)

  • Purpose: Present completed work from the sprint to stakeholders and gather feedback.
