AI Investigation : Malicious Uses of GPT-4

Created by Josslyne Shantz (Unlicensed) on May 30, 2024

There are some malicious use cases for GPT-4 and AI in general. We will be focusing on GPT-4 as that is one of the LLMs (Large Language Models) that Microsoft Copilot uses.

Hacking

Researchers at the University of Illinois found that GPT-4 can be used to create exploits in known security vulnerabilities. During their research, they tested GPT-4’s capabilities with 15 vulnerabilities one Mitre’s list of Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (CVEs). GPT-4 had an 87% success rate in exploiting the vulnerabilities based on CVE data.
