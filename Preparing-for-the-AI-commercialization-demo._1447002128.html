This demo is aimed at showcasing our capabilities to the Large AI Committee while highlighting the commercialization potential of the generative AI chatbot we are developing. The demo is divided into Level 1 and Level 2, with the levels defined based on Sean's article.

DTA Scale for AI in Education

The DTA (Digital Teaching Assistant) scale provides a roadmap for the progression of AI capabilities in education, similar to how the SAE scale tracks self-driving car advancements. It helps set clear expectations for integrating AI into education, from simple informational support to personalized coaching.

DTA Cognitive Autonomy Scale:

  • Level 0 - No DTA: No AI assistance, entirely human-managed.

  • Level 1 - Informational DTA: Answers transactional questions based on course content.

  • Level 2 - Interpretive DTA: Synthesizes course content to answer abstract questions.

  • Level 3 - Responsive Coaching DTA: Provides learning strategies and resource information.

  • Level 4 - Personal Coaching DTA: Creates interactive content, manages course load, and tailors interactions based on student needs.

  • Level 5 - Journey Coaching DTA: Integrates program-wide data to provide holistic advice on courses and career goals.

The scale ensures that AI enhances learning at each level, with data playing a crucial role in customization. Responsible data usage and privacy are key for the development and deployment of DTAs.

Technical flow

The code used in this demo is based on the work done during the S24 term. The frontend and backend were developed using Angular, while Olama Embedding was used for model embedding, and the LLM (Large Language Model) utilized is Mistral. The database used in the frontend is PostgreSQL, which manages course-related data.
